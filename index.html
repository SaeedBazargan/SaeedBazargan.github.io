About me

Welcome to my page! I am an aspiring robotics engineer with a passion for innovation and technology. With over ten years of experience in international robotics competitions, I have accumulated a wealth of knowledge and a trove of awards that reflect my commitment to excellence in the field.

From the bustling labs of Iran, my career has been a blend of rigorous research and hands-on engineering. I specialize in designing complex high-frequency PCBs and implementing advanced encryption protocols to enhance data security. My deep dive into electronics and programming has led me to develop functional and revolutionary applications in robotics and IoT. My robust experience includes leading the electronics division of a humanoid soccer robot team, achieving multiple victories in the RoboCup. I thrive in high-stress environments, ensuring project success through meticulous planning and problem-solving. My technical toolkit is rich with proficiencies in C, Python, and C#.NET, alongside expertise in frontrunners like STM32, ARM Cortex-M, and AVR microcontrollers.
