USB2Dynamixel Design

Engineering:

Design a modified version of the USB2Dynamixel (USB2DXL) board, which is the interface between the Dynamixel actuators and the controller. This board is designed to communicate with both TTL and RS-485 Dynamixel series at the same time. Multiple iterations of the PCB were developed, enhancing my fundamental skills in PCB design.

Skills used

  • Circuit Analysis

  • DipTrace

  • PCB Design

  • Phys/Mathematics
